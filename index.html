Habit Tracker

Privacy Policy

Habit Tracker is built privacy-first. Your habits stay on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

Effective May 14, 2026 com.viewventure.habittracker

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All habits, completions, reminders, and preferences are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

  • No account creation or sign-in.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • No network requests carrying user data.

Permissions Used

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — deliver habit reminders at your chosen times.
  • Exact alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM) — fire reminders at the precise scheduled time.
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — restore reminders after device reboot.
  • Wake lock (WAKE_LOCK) — briefly wake the device when a reminder fires.

Data Storage

Your data lives in a local database on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all data. The App does not provide cloud backup or export. Standard Android system backups may include the App's data if you have enabled them in device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from anyone, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or backend services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page.
